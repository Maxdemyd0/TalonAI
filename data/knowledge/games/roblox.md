# Roblox

Roblox is a platform where players can access many user-created games and social experiences.

## Core Identity

- Platform-driven
- Social
- User-generated
- Broad in style and genre

## Main Activities

- Playing games made by other users
- Joining friends in shared experiences
- Exploring different genres such as roleplay, obbies, shooters, and simulators
- Creating games and systems with Roblox development tools

## What Makes It Distinct

- It is less like one single game and more like a large ecosystem of games
- The content range is wide because creators build very different experiences
- The platform mixes play, creation, and community

## Why People Play It

- Huge variety of experiences
- Easy social play
- A path from player to creator
- Constant novelty from new user-made content
