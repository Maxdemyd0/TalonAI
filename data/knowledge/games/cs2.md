# Counter-Strike 2

Counter-Strike 2, often called CS2, is a tactical first-person shooter focused on team coordination, economy management, and precise gunplay.

## Core Identity

- Tactical
- Competitive
- High-skill
- Round-based

## Main Activities

- Attacking or defending bomb sites
- Managing money across rounds
- Trading utility and positioning advantages
- Winning through aim, teamwork, timing, and map control

## What Makes It Distinct

- Small mistakes are often punished quickly
- Utility usage is as important as raw aim
- Team communication and map knowledge have a huge impact
- The round structure creates constant pressure and adaptation

## Why People Play It

- Deep competitive mastery
- Strong emphasis on teamwork
- Satisfying precision shooting
- Clear skill expression through strategy and mechanics
