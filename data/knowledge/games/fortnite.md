# Fortnite

Fortnite is a multiplayer action game developed by Epic Games.

## Core Identity

- Fast-paced
- Colorful
- Competitive
- Social

## Main Modes

- Battle royale matches with a shrinking safe zone
- Creative experiences made by players and studios
- Cooperative and casual modes depending on the experience

## What Makes It Distinct

- Building and editing structures can change fights quickly
- Movement and aiming are both important
- The game often mixes combat with events, cosmetics, and user-made content

## Why People Play It

- Quick matches
- Constant variety
- Strong social play with friends
- A blend of shooting, movement, and improvisation
