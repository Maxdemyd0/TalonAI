# Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game centered on exploration, building, mining, and survival.

## Core Identity

- Open-ended
- Creative
- Relaxed or challenging depending on the mode
- Strongly shaped by player goals

## Main Activities

- Gathering resources
- Crafting tools and blocks
- Building structures
- Exploring caves, villages, and other areas
- Surviving hostile creatures in survival mode

## What Makes It Distinct

- The block-based world makes construction intuitive
- Players can focus on creativity, automation, survival, or adventure
- Mods, maps, and servers can change the game dramatically

## Why People Play It

- Freedom to build almost anything
- Long-term progression in survival worlds
- Cooperative building and server communities
- A strong mix of creativity and discovery
