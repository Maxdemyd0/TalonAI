# Plants

Plants are living organisms that usually make their own food through photosynthesis.

## Core Ideas

- Most plants use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide to produce energy.
- Plants release oxygen as part of photosynthesis.
- Roots absorb water and nutrients from the soil.
- Stems help support the plant and move water and nutrients.
- Leaves are often the main place where photosynthesis happens.

## Why Plants Matter

- Plants are a foundation of many food chains.
- They help produce oxygen for many living things.
- They provide food, wood, fibers, and medicine.
- They help shape habitats for animals and insects.

## Common Plant Types

- Trees
- Flowers
- Grasses
- Shrubs
- Ferns
- Mosses

## Growth

Plants need the right combination of light, water, nutrients, temperature, and space to grow well.
