# Humans

Humans are highly social mammals known for language, reasoning, creativity, and complex societies.

## Core Ideas

- Humans are animals, but they also build advanced cultures, tools, and technologies.
- Human societies are shaped by communication, cooperation, and learning.
- People live in many different environments and cultures around the world.

## Human Strengths

- Language and symbolic thinking
- Problem solving
- Cooperation in large groups
- Tool creation
- Long-term planning

## Daily Human Needs

- Food
- Water
- Shelter
- Safety
- Health care
- Social connection

## Society

- Humans form families, communities, and governments.
- Education helps pass knowledge from one generation to the next.
- Work, trade, and technology help societies function and grow.

## Challenges

- Disease
- Conflict
- Resource management
- Inequality
- Environmental impact
