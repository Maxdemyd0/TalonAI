# Animals

Animals are living organisms that eat other organisms or organic material for energy.

## Core Ideas

- Animals can move at some stage of life.
- Animals need food, water, and suitable habitats.
- Different animals have different body structures and survival strategies.
- Many animals depend on plants or other animals for food.

## Major Groups

- Mammals
- Birds
- Fish
- Reptiles
- Amphibians
- Insects

## Behavior

- Some animals live alone, while others live in groups.
- Animals may hunt, graze, scavenge, migrate, or hibernate.
- Many animals use sound, movement, scent, or color to communicate.

## Why Animals Matter

- Animals are part of ecosystems and food webs.
- Some animals help pollinate plants.
- Some spread seeds or control pest populations.
- Humans also rely on animals for companionship, food, work, and research.
