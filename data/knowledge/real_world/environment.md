# Environment

The environment includes the natural surroundings and conditions in which living things exist.

## Core Ideas

- The environment includes air, water, land, climate, and ecosystems.
- Living things depend on environmental conditions to survive.
- Changes in one part of an ecosystem can affect many other parts.

## Important Parts

- Atmosphere
- Oceans and rivers
- Soil
- Forests
- Grasslands
- Deserts
- Polar regions

## Environmental Issues

- Pollution
- Deforestation
- Habitat loss
- Climate change
- Water scarcity
- Biodiversity decline

## Why It Matters

- Healthy environments support life.
- Clean air and water are essential for health.
- Stable ecosystems help provide food and natural resources.
- Environmental protection helps both humans and wildlife.
