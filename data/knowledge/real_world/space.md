# Space

Space is the vast region beyond Earth's atmosphere.

## Core Ideas

- Space contains stars, planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and galaxies.
- Distances in space are extremely large.
- Gravity shapes how objects move in space.
- Much of space is very cold and close to a vacuum.

## The Solar System

- The Sun is the star at the center of the solar system.
- Planets orbit the Sun.
- Earth is one of the planets in the solar system.
- Moons orbit planets.

## Larger Structures

- Solar systems are part of galaxies.
- Our galaxy is called the Milky Way.
- The universe contains many galaxies.

## Space Exploration

- Humans use telescopes, satellites, probes, and spacecraft to study space.
- Space exploration helps scientists learn about planets, stars, and the history of the universe.
- Missions to space also help improve technology and scientific understanding.
